Welcome to Climate Change Awareness

Climate change is one of the most pressing issues of our time. Learn more about its causes, effects, and solutions.

Causes of Climate Change

The main causes of climate change include burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial processes, leading to increased greenhouse gas emissions.

Effects of Climate Change

Rising global temperatures, melting polar ice caps, and extreme weather events are some of the key impacts of climate change.

Solutions to Climate Change

Adopting renewable energy, reducing waste, and promoting sustainable practices are crucial in combating climate change.
